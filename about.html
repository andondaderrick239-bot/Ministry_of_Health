About Ministry of Health

Who We Are

The Ministry of Health leads Uganda's health sector through policy, planning, and oversight. We support programs that protect public health, expand access to services, and improve wellbeing for every citizen.

Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality health services that promote, prevent, cure, and rehabilitate health conditions for all Ugandans.

Vision

A healthy population supported by an efficient, sustainable, and equitable health system.
