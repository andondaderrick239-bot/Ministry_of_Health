Health Officer Recruitment

Job Overview

The Ministry of Health Uganda invites qualified and motivated Ugandans to apply for Health Officer positions to support quality healthcare service delivery across government health facilities.

Job Title
Health Officer
Location
Various Districts in Uganda
Application Deadline
30 July 2026

Key Responsibilities

  • Assess and manage patients
  • Maintain accurate medical records

Minimum Qualifications

  • Registered with relevant professional council
  • Valid practicing license
  • Computer literacy is an added advantage
