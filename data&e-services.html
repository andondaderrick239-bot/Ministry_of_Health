Welcome to Data & E-Services

The Ministry of Health Uganda provides digital services, health information resources, facility information systems, learning platforms and access to national health data.


Uganda has made progress in reducing communicable diseases, improving maternal and child health services, increasing immunization coverage and strengthening disease surveillance. The health sector continues to focus on malaria control, HIV/AIDS prevention, tuberculosis management, emergency preparedness, nutrition and universal health coverage.


This portal provides access to reports, guidelines, educational resources, health facility information, electronic learning and partner coordination services.
