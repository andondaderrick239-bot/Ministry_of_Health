Procurement & Tenders

Ministry of Health Uganda

Overview

The Procurement & Tenders section provides information on available procurement opportunities, invitations to bid, and contract awards. Suppliers and service providers are encouraged to submit competitive bids in accordance with procurement guidelines.

Current Opportunities

  • Supply of Medical Equipment
  • Supply of Office Furniture
  • ICT Equipment and Software
  • Construction and Renovation Works
  • Consultancy Services

Requirements

  • Valid company registration
  • Current tax clearance certificate
  • Relevant experience in the required field
  • Submission of all required bidding documents

Application Deadline

31 July 2026
