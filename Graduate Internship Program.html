Graduate Internship Program

Ministry of Health Uganda

Program Overview

The Graduate Internship Program provides recent graduates with practical experience, professional mentorship, and career development opportunities across various departments within the Ministry of Health Uganda.

Eligibility

  • Must have completed a Bachelor’s Degree
  • Graduated within the last two years
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills
  • Willingness to learn and grow professionally

Duration

12 months

Application Deadline

31 July 2026
