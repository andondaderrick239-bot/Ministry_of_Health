Scholarships & Training Programs

Ministry of Health Uganda

Program Overview

Our Scholarships and Training Programs support students and professionals by providing educational funding, specialized training, and capacity-building opportunities to enhance skills and career development within Uganda’s health sector.

Available Opportunities

  • Undergraduate Scholarships
  • Postgraduate Scholarships
  • Professional Development Courses
  • Health Sector Training Workshops
  • Leadership and Management Training

Eligibility

  • Must meet academic requirements
  • Demonstrate commitment to professional growth
  • Possess relevant educational qualifications
  • Submit all required application documents

Duration

12 months

Application Deadline

31 July 2026
